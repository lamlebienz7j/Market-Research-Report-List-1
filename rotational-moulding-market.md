<p><p>Rotational moulding is a manufacturing process used to produce hollow plastic products by heating plastic powder in a rotating mould. As the mould rotates, the plastic evenly coats the interior surface, resulting in a uniform thickness and seamless design. This technique is highly versatile, enabling the production of items ranging from storage tanks and kayaks to toys and medical devices.</p><p>The Rotational Moulding Market is poised for significant growth, with expectations of a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period. This expansion can be attributed to increasing demand across various industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods. The lightweight and durable nature of rotationally moulded products is driving their adoption in products requiring excellent strength-to-weight ratios.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards sustainability, with manufacturers exploring eco-friendly materials and processes to reduce environmental impact. Additionally, innovations in technology, such as automation and improved mould designs, are enhancing production efficiency and product quality.
