<p><p>Multimode optical fiber market includes different types based on core sizes, primarily 50/125μm and 62.5/125μm fibers. Conversely, 62.5/125μm fibers, with a larger core, are typically used in legacy systems and are suited for short-distance applications. Both fiber types cater to diverse networking needs, influencing market dynamics based on industry requirements and technological advancements.</p></p>
