<p><p>The Cytomegalovirus (CMV) assay market comprises several key types, including Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), PCR assays, DNA assays, and others. DNA assays analyze specific segments of viral DNA, providing insights into viral load and strain.
