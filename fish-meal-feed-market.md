<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD)</li><li>Flame Dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fish meal feed market primarily consists of two types: Steam Dried (SD) and Flame Dried (FD) fish meals. SD fish meal is produced using steam under high pressure, which preserves nutrients and enhances digestibility, making it suitable for high-quality aquaculture feeds. In contrast, FD fish meal involves direct exposure to flames, resulting in a distinct flavor and potentially lower nutrient retention. Both types cater to different segments of the aquaculture industry based on nutritional requirements and cost considerations.</p></p>
