<p><ul><li>First Stage Scuba Regulator</li><li>Second Stage Scuba Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scuba regulator market can be categorized into two primary types: first-stage and second-stage regulators. The first-stage regulator connects to the scuba tank and reduces high-pressure air to an intermediate pressure, making it manageable for breathing. The second-stage regulator further reduces this pressure and delivers the air to the diver on demand. Both types are essential for safe underwater breathing, and they vary in design, performance, and features, catering to different levels of diving enthusiasts and professionals.</p></p>
