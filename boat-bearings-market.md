<p><strong>The Boat Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat bearings market is primarily divided into two types: rolling bearings and sliding bearings. Rolling bearings utilize spherical elements to reduce friction between moving parts, offering high efficiency and load-carrying capacity, making them suitable for high-speed applications in marine environments. Sliding bearings, on the other hand, rely on a smooth surface to support loads, providing enhanced durability in low-speed and high-load scenarios. Both types are crucial in ensuring reliable performance and longevity of boat machinery and components.</p></p>
