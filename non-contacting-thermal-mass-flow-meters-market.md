<p><ul><li>Thermal Dispersion Type</li><li>Boundary Layer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contacting thermal mass flow meters measure flow rates without direct contact with the fluid. The thermal dispersion type utilizes heated elements and measures temperature differentials to determine mass flow by analyzing how heat dissipates in the medium. In contrast, the boundary layer type focuses on the thermal interactions within the flow's boundary layer, determining flow rate based on changes in thermal resistance and temperature profiles. Both types are valuable for applications requiring precise flow measurement without altering the flow system.</p></p>
