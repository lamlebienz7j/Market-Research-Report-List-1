<p><strong>The Glass Ionomer Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Glass Ionomer Restoratives</li><li>Resin Modified Glass Ionomer Restoratives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Ionomer Cement market is primarily categorized into Conventional Glass Ionomer Restoratives and Resin Modified Glass Ionomer Restoratives. Conventional Glass Ionomer Restoratives are used for direct bonding to tooth structure, offering fluoride release and minimal thermal expansion, ideal for non-load-bearing restorations. In contrast, Resin Modified Glass Ionomer Restoratives incorporate resin components, enhancing mechanical properties and aesthetics, making them suitable for a wider range of applications, including posterior restorations and areas requiring better wear resistance. Both types play critical roles in dental restorative practices.</p></p>
