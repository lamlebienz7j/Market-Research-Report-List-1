<p><p>The 3D cell culture scaffold market includes three main types: hydrogels, fibers, and other materials. Hydrogels are water-based, biocompatible networks that provide a moist environment, mimicking natural tissue for cell growth. Fibers, often made from polymers, offer structural support and can be engineered for specific mechanical properties. Other market types encompass diverse materials, including sponges and matrices, that facilitate varying cell interactions and functionalities. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing tissue engineering and regenerative medicine applications.</p></p>
