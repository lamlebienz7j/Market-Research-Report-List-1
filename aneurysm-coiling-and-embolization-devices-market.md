<p><strong>The Aneurysm Coiling and Embolization Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices encompass various tools used to treat cerebral aneurysms. Embolization coils are soft metal coils that occlude blood flow to the aneurysm. Balloon and stent-assisted coils help secure coils in place. Access and delivery devices facilitate the placement of these coils. Aneurysm clips provide surgical clipping of the aneurysm neck, while flow diversion devices redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm, reducing rupture risk. These devices are crucial for effective aneurysm management.</p></p>
