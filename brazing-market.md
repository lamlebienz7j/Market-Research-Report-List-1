<p><p>Brazing is a metal-joining process that involves melting a filler metal to join two or more workpieces together without melting the base materials. The filler metal has a melting point above 450°C and is drawn into the joint by capillary action, forming a solid bond upon cooling. This technique offers advantages such as strong joints, the ability to join dissimilar metals, and a reduced risk of thermal distortion.</p><p>The Brazing Market is experiencing significant growth driven by increasing demand in industries such as aerospace, automotive, electronics, and HVAC. The need for lightweight and durable components has intensified, leading manufacturers to adopt brazing for its strength and versatility. Key trends influencing the market include advancements in brazing materials, automation of brazing processes, and an increased focus on eco-friendly solutions. </p><p>The Brazing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period, reflecting the growing importance of reliable joining solutions in modern manufacturing.
