<p><p>The coating additives market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Acrylics** enhance the durability and gloss of coatings, providing weather resistance and UV protection.</p><p>2. **Fluoropolymers** offer superior chemical resistance and non-stick properties, often used in high-performance applications.</p><p>3. **Urethanes** improve flexibility and toughness, making coatings more resilient to wear and tear.</p><p>4. **Metallic additives** add aesthetic appeal and improved conductivity, often used in specialty coatings for both functional and decorative purposes. Each type serves specific roles in enhancing performance and application.</p></p>
