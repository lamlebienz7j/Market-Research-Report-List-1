<p><p>The Direct Attach Copper Cable (DAC) market encompasses various types of connectors designed for high-speed data transmission in data centers and networking. SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) cables are compact and widely used for short distances. SFP+ cables offer higher bandwidth, suitable for 10 Gigabit Ethernet. QSFP/QSFP+ supports even greater speeds for multi-channel applications. XFP cables accommodate 10 Gigabit data rates, while CXP provides connections for 40 Gigabit systems. Other types include specialized connectors and hybrid options catering to diverse networking needs.</p></p>
