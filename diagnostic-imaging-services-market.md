<p><p>The Diagnostic Imaging Services market is categorized into several key types. Diagnostic Radiology encompasses traditional imaging techniques like X-rays. Ultrasound uses sound waves for real-time imaging, while Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-rays and computer processing for detailed cross-sectional views. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) utilizes strong magnetic fields and radio waves for high-resolution images of soft tissues. Nuclear Imaging involves radioactive substances to visualize organ function. "Others" may include modalities like PET scans or specialized imaging techniques, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
