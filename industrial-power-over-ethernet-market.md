<p><p>The Industrial Power Over Ethernet (PoE) market is categorized into Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) and Powered Devices (PD). PSE includes devices like PoE switches and injectors that supply power and data over Ethernet cables, facilitating installations without separate power sources. PD encompasses the end devices, such as IP cameras, VoIP phones, and wireless access points, that receive power and data through the same cable. This integration enhances efficiency and simplifies network deployments in industrial environments.</p></p>
