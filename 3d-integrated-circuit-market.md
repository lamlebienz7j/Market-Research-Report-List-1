<p><p>The 3D Integrated Circuit (IC) market includes three main types: Through Silicon Via (TSV), Silicon Interposer, and Through Glass Via (TGV). TSV utilizes vertical interconnections within silicon wafers, enabling high-density circuitry and improved performance. Silicon interposers serve as a middle layer that connects multiple chips, enhancing communication while reducing signal loss. TGV employs glass substrates for vertical connections, offering lower capacitance and improved thermal performance. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing functionality and efficiency in advanced electronic systems.</p></p>
