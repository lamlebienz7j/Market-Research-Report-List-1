<p><p>The nickel paste market is categorized into three main types based on sintering temperature: Low Temperature Sintered Nickel Paste, Medium Temperature Sintered Nickel Paste, and High Temperature Sintered Nickel Paste. Low-temperature variants typically sinter at lower thermal profiles, suitable for delicate substrates. Medium-temperature types balance performance and thermal management, while high-temperature sintered pastes exceed higher thresholds for demanding applications. Each type serves distinct applications in electronics, batteries, and catalysis, driven by the required thermal and mechanical properties.</p></p>
