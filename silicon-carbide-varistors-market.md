<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Varistor market is primarily divided into two types: Tube Varistors and Disc Varistors. Tube Varistors are cylindrical devices offering high energy absorption and are typically used in applications requiring robust voltage clamping. Conversely, Disc Varistors are characterized by their flat, disc-shaped design, which provides superior performance in applications with limited space. Both types serve as protective components in electrical circuits, effectively diverting excess voltage during transients to safeguard sensitive equipment.</p></p>
