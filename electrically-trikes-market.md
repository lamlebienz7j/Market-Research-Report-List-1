<p><p>The electric trike market is categorized mainly into two types based on motor placement: side wheel motors and center motors. Side wheel motors provide added stability and balance, making them ideal for casual riders and those who prioritize ease of use. In contrast, center motors offer better weight distribution and improved handling, appealing to riders seeking performance and agility. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, with side motor trikes focusing on comfort and center motor trikes emphasizing efficiency and control.</p></p>
