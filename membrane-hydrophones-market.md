<p><p>The membrane hydrophones market features two main types: differential membrane hydrophones and co-planar membrane hydrophones. Differential membrane hydrophones utilize a dual-membrane design to enhance sensitivity and reduce noise, making them ideal for precise underwater acoustic measurements. Co-planar membrane hydrophones, on the other hand, have a single membrane configuration that provides a simpler design, suitable for various applications including environmental monitoring and underwater communication. Both types cater to diverse needs in marine acoustics and sensing technologies.</p></p>
