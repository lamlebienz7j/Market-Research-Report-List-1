<p><p>Dolby Vision TVs primarily fall into three market types: OLED, LED, and LCD. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) TVs offer superior contrast and vibrant colors due to self-emissive pixels, making them ideal for deep blacks and wide viewing angles. LED (Light Emitting Diode) TVs use a backlight with liquid crystal displays, providing bright images and energy efficiency. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) TVs are generally more affordable and deliver decent picture quality, but may lack the enhanced color depth and contrast of OLED models in Dolby Vision.</p></p>
