<p><p>The artist-grade acrylic paints market consists primarily of fluid acrylics and heavy-body acrylics. Fluid acrylics are characterized by their smooth, pourable consistency, ideal for techniques requiring fine detail and blending, such as glazing and washes. In contrast, heavy-body acrylics have a thicker texture, allowing for expressive brushwork and impasto effects. Both types offer high pigment concentration and lightfast properties, catering to professional artists' needs for vibrant color and durability across various creative applications.</p></p>
