<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The High-Speed 3D Profile Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Triangulation Sensors</li><li>Structured Light Projection Sensors</li><li>Time-of-Flight (ToF) Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed 3D profile sensor market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Laser Triangulation Sensors** utilize a laser beam to measure surfaces by calculating angles, delivering high precision for small objects.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Structured Light Projection Sensors** project a known pattern onto an object and capture its deformation, enabling detailed shape recognition.</p><p>3. **Time-of-Flight (ToF) Sensors** measure the time it takes for emitted light to return, providing fast and accurate depth information across larger areas. Each type serves unique industrial applications.</p></p>
