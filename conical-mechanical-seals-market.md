<p><p>Conical mechanical seals are essential components used to prevent leaks in rotating equipment. The market can be divided into two main types: pusher type seals, which utilize a flexible element that maintains contact with the sealing surfaces as wear occurs, ensuring effective sealing under varying conditions; and non-pusher seals, which rely on a stationary design without adjustable elements. Each type serves distinct applications, with pusher seals typically offering better adaptability in dynamic situations compared to their non-pusher counterparts.</p></p>
