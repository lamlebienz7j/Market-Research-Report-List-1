<p><p>The Open Modular Building market is divided into two main types: Permanent Modular and Relocatable Modular. Permanent Modular buildings are constructed off-site and designed for long-term use, featuring a durable structure and often integrated with utilities, ideal for schools and healthcare facilities. In contrast, Relocatable Modular buildings are designed for temporary use, allowing easy transport and reconfiguration, making them suitable for construction sites, seasonal needs, or emergency response. Both types offer flexibility and efficiency in construction methods.</p></p>
