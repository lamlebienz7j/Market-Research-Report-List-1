<p><p>The smart bidet market is divided into two primary types: one-piece and split-type smart bidets. One-piece smart bidets feature an integrated toilet and bidet in a seamless design, offering convenience and aesthetic appeal. They are often easier to clean and require less installation space. In contrast, split-type smart bidets consist of a separate bidet seat that can be added to existing toilets, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types enhance bathroom hygiene and comfort through advanced features.</p></p>
