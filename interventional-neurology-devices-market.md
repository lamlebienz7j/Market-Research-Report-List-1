<p><strong>The Interventional Neurology Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aneurysm Coiling and Embolization Devices</li><li>Angioplasty Devices</li><li>Micro Support Devices</li><li>Neurothrombectomy Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Interventional Neurology Devices market encompasses several key types. Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used to treat brain aneurysms by blocking blood flow to the affected vessel. Angioplasty devices facilitate the widening of narrowed cerebral arteries, restoring blood flow. Micro support devices assist in the navigation of delicate anatomy during procedures. Neurothrombectomy devices are employed to remove blood clots from cerebral vessels, thereby preventing or mitigating strokes. Together, these devices enhance treatment options for neurological conditions.</p></p>
