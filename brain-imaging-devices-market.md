<p><p>The brain imaging devices market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **CT Devices** utilize X-ray technology to create detailed cross-sectional images of the brain, aiding in diagnosing injuries and conditions.</p><p> </p><p>2. **MRI Devices** use magnetic fields and radio waves to produce high-resolution images of brain structures, crucial for detecting tumors and neurological disorders.</p><p>3. **PET Devices** assess metabolic activity in the brain using radioactive tracers, helping identify abnormalities.</p><p>4. **PET-CT and PET-MRI Devices** combine the imaging capabilities of PET with CT or MRI, offering comprehensive insights into both function and structure for enhanced diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
