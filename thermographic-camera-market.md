<p><p>The thermographic camera market features two primary types of infrared detectors: cooled and uncooled. Cooled infrared detectors employ advanced cryogenic technology to reduce sensor temperature, enhancing sensitivity and precision in detecting thermal radiation. They are suitable for high-performance applications, such as military and scientific research. Uncooled infrared detectors, on the other hand, operate at ambient temperatures, making them more compact and cost-effective, ideal for commercial and industrial uses, such as building inspections and automotive applications.</p></p>
