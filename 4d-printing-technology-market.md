<p><strong>The 4D Printing Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet Printing</li><li>Melt Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Direct Ink Writing (DIW)</li><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Electron Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4D printing technology market encompasses various techniques including inkjet printing, which layers materials to create intricate designs; melt deposition modeling (FDM), utilizing thermoplastic filaments layer by layer for flexibility; direct ink writing (DIW), which extrudes functional inks to form complex geometries; stereolithography (SLA), employing UV light to cure photopolymer resins; electron beam melting (EBM), involving high-energy electron beams to fuse metal powder; and selective laser melting (SLM), which uses lasers for precise metal part fabrication. Each method offers unique capabilities for dynamic material behavior and structural transformation over time.</p></p>
