<p><p>The Laparoscopic Retrieval Bag market is categorized into two main types: Manual Opening and Automatic Opening. Manual Opening bags require the surgeon to manually open the bag to retrieve specimens, offering precise control during procedures. In contrast, Automatic Opening bags use mechanisms that allow them to open automatically when deployed, enhancing efficiency and reducing the risk of contamination. Both types cater to different surgical preferences, impacting operational workflow and overall patient outcomes in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
