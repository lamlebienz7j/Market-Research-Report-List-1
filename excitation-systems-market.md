<p><ul><li>Static Excitation Systems</li><li>Brushless Excitation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Excitation systems are critical for regulating voltage and reactive power in power generation. Static excitation systems utilize solid-state devices to control the excitation of generators, offering rapid response and minimal maintenance. In contrast, brushless excitation systems employ rotating components that eliminate the need for brushes, enhancing reliability and reducing wear. Both types cater to different market needs: static systems provide precision and efficiency, while brushless systems emphasize durability and low maintenance, making them suitable for various power generation applications.</p></p>
