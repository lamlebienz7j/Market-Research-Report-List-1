<p><p>The medical cryogenic storage containers market encompasses various types designed for preserving biological samples. Medical refrigerators maintain a temperature range of 2°C to 8°C, suitable for short-term storage of pharmaceuticals. Blood refrigerators specifically store blood products at 4°C ± 1°C. Biomedical freezers operate between -10°C and -50°C for long-term preservation. Ultra-low freezers maintain temperatures from -50°C to -150°C for sensitive materials, while liquid nitrogen tanks, ranging from -150°C to -196°C, are ideal for cryopreservation of cells and tissues.</p></p>
