<p><p>High Flow Oxygen Therapy Devices are categorized into two main types: Automatic Oxygen Adjustment and Manual Oxygen Adjustment. Automatic Oxygen Adjustment devices automatically regulate oxygen concentration based on the patient's breathing patterns and needs, enhancing comfort and efficiency. In contrast, Manual Oxygen Adjustment devices require healthcare providers or patients to manually set the oxygen flow levels, allowing for tailored treatment but potentially being less responsive to immediate changes in oxygen requirements. Both types serve distinct patient needs in respiratory care.</p></p>
