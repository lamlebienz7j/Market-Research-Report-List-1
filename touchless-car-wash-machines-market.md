<p><strong>The Touchless Car Wash Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tunnel Type</li><li>Rollover Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Touchless car wash machines come in two main types: tunnel type and rollover type. Tunnel type systems utilize a conveyor belt to move vehicles through a series of cleaning stations, leveraging high-pressure jets and specialized cleaning solutions for a thorough wash without brushes. Rollover type systems feature stationary cleaning units that move over the vehicle while it remains parked, providing an efficient wash experience. Both types aim to deliver effective cleaning while minimizing contact to reduce the risk of scratches and damage to the vehicle's surface.</p></p>
