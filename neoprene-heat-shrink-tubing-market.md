<p><p>Neoprene heat shrink tubing is available in two primary types: round tube and flat tube. Round tube is designed for insulating and protecting wires and cables, providing a snug fit when heated, which enhances durability and flexibility. Flat tube, on the other hand, is ideal for covering flat or irregular-shaped components, offering versatility in applications like electrical insulation or strain relief. Both types are valued for their resistance to chemicals, abrasion, and environmental factors, making them suitable for various industrial uses.</p></p>
