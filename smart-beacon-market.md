<p><p>The Smart Beacon market primarily comprises two types: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and Hybrid beacons. BLE beacons utilize Bluetooth technology to transmit signals to nearby devices, enabling location-based services, proximity marketing, and tracking with minimal energy consumption. Hybrid beacons combine BLE with other technologies, such as Wi-Fi or RFID, allowing for enhanced functionality and broader use cases. This versatility caters to diverse applications, from retail and logistics to event management, optimizing user engagement and improving operational efficiency.</p></p>
