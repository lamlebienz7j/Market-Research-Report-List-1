<p><p>The diesel aircraft tugs market comprises two main types: conventional tractors and towbarless tractors. Conventional tractors are equipped with tow bars and are commonly used for moving aircraft with a traditional hitch system. In contrast, towbarless tractors connect directly to the aircraft's nose gear, allowing for more maneuverability and direct operation, eliminating the need for towing bars. Both types serve to enhance ground handling efficiency, improve safety, and facilitate aircraft movement in busy airport environments.</p></p>
