<p><p>The Implantable Cardiac Rhythm Management Device market encompasses various types of devices that assist in monitoring and regulating heart rhythms. Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillators (ICDs) are designed to detect and correct life-threatening arrhythmias by delivering shocks when necessary. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices help improve heart function in patients with heart failure by synchronizing the contractions of the heart's chambers. Each device plays a crucial role in enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
