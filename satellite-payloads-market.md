<p><p>The satellite payloads market is categorized into three primary orbit types: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Geosynchronous Earth Orbit (GEO), and Medium Earth Orbit (MEO). LEO satellites provide low-latency communication and are ideal for Earth observation and broadband services. GEO satellites maintain a constant position relative to the Earth, which is advantageous for television broadcasting and weather monitoring. MEO satellites balance coverage and latency, commonly used for navigation systems such as GPS. Each type serves distinct applications and market needs.</p></p>
