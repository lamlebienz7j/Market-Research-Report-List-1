<p><strong>The Anti Static Bar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Inductive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-static bar market comprises two primary types: contact type and inductive type. Contact type anti-static bars function by directly touching the surfaces to eliminate static charges, making them effective for specific applications like packaging and electronics. In contrast, inductive type anti-static bars generate a static charge without direct contact, utilizing an ionizing process that neutralizes static fields in larger areas. Both types are essential for reducing static electricity in various industries, enhancing product quality and safety.</p></p>
