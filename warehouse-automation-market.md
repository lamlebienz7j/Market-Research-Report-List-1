<p><p>The warehouse automation market encompasses various technologies designed to enhance efficiency and productivity. Goods-To-Person (GTP) systems streamline order fulfillment by transporting items directly to operators, reducing travel time. Pick-To-Light systems utilize lights to guide pickers, improving accuracy and speed. Sortation systems automate the sorting process of items for shipping, increasing throughput. Collaborative Mobile Robots assist human workers by transporting goods within the warehouse, while other market segments include automated storage and retrieval systems, enhancing overall operational performance.</p></p>
