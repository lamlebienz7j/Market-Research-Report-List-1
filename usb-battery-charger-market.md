<p><strong>The USB Battery Charger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Downstream Port (SDP)</li><li>Charging Downstream Port (CDP)</li><li>Dedicated Charging Port (DCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB battery charger market includes three main types: Standard Downstream Port (SDP), Charging Downstream Port (CDP), and Dedicated Charging Port (DCP). SDP supplies limited power suitable for data transfer and standard device charging. CDP allows for faster charging while supporting data transfer, using a more advanced detection mechanism. DCP, on the other hand, is designed solely for charging, delivering higher power without data capabilities, making it ideal for rapid charging of devices like smartphones and tablets.</p></p>
