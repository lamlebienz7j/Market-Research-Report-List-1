<p><p>The bio-absorbable bone screw market is categorized into fully threaded screws and partially threaded screws. Fully threaded screws feature threads along their entire length, providing enhanced stability and fixation in various bone applications. In contrast, partially threaded screws have a smooth shaft section, allowing for easier insertion and better alignment while maintaining adequate grip. Both types are designed for temporary fixation, gradually absorbing into the body, thus eliminating the need for subsequent removal surgeries while promoting healing.</p></p>
