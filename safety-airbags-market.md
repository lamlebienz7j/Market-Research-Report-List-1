<p><p>The safety airbags market encompasses two main types: single airbags and multi airbags. Single airbags typically include one deploying unit (often in the steering wheel), primarily protecting the driver during collisions. In contrast, multi airbags involve multiple deployment units positioned throughout the vehicle, such as side, curtain, and knee airbags, offering comprehensive protection for all occupants. The multi airbags market is gaining traction due to increasing safety regulations and consumer demand for enhanced vehicle safety features.</p></p>
